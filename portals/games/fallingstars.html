Falling Stars [PICO.SB2]

A spin-off of Bird & Beans for Nintendo DSi, made in Scratch.

How to play

  1. The game may not show up right away. You may have to enable unauthorized scripts in your browser.
  2. Click on the flag to begin the game. If you want to skip the intro scene, press [space].
  3. Click on "Free Play" or "Infinite Mode" to begin the game.
  4. In either game mode, press 'A' and 'D' to move Pico left or right.
  5. Press 'W' to make Pico jump.
  6. Click the left mouse button to shoot watermelons at a 45-degree angle upwards..
  7. Your goal is to hit the stars before they touch the ground. When the stars touch the ground, the area that they touch disappears.
  8. The game ends when Pico is hit by a star or when he falls through the ground.
  9. Click on the flag again to restart the game!

Tips

  • You may not be able to hit the stars the first time, so be sure to aim!
  • You can hold the left mouse button to release a barrage of watermelons.
  • Even if a platform disappears, you can jump to them!
  • Infinite Mode is the same thing as Free Play, but without a one-minute timer.
  • Sometimes Pico gets stuck in the ground. When that happens, jump through the side outwards and try to get back on top.
