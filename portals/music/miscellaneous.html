84-GWAM

In late 2016, my friends and I formed a band for a state-wide music production competition. I was the leader, so my friends took my average typing speed at the time (80 gross words a minute), and because there were four people in the band, they added four to it. So we became 84-GWAM.

Members

Songs Made For School

Sometimes, whenever there's a project for a class, the teacher allows me to make a song for it. Of course, it still has to cover the same topics as the other project formats, but I have the advantage when it comes to making music. Here are the instrumentals of the songs I made for school.

Devil in Disguise

This song was made for a metaphor project
comparing love to something else in Literature
class. We've also read lots of tragic stories
surrounding love in the class, so I compared it
to a devil in disguise.

Clone Me

In biology class, the teacher assigned us a
project to research gene technology. I chose one
of my classmates as a parter, and made a song
about cloning.
