About This Year

I got a lot accomplished in 2016. Two of my albums, Synchronized and K-Step Techno: EVOLUTION, were released. Not only that, I had developed a new style that improved my music quality.

I also made a lot of singles this year, but many of them were unreleased to the public due to various reasons. Here are the released singles for 2016.

K-Step

This was a short song was made using samples
from sampulator.com. I suggest playing around
with the website!

tonematrix - ONELOOP [Flowey]

2016 was mainly a year of experimentation,
and I found audiotool.com one day. I used
one of its instruments, tonematrix, and composed
a repeating melody by drawing "Flowey" in a
16x16 grid.

Steel Force

I think this was the best song I've ever made
since Zero Matrix, and this was published on
December 31, 2016. Different from my other
songs, Steel Force was the first song that was
not an instrumental.
