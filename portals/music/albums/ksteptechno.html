K-Step Techno: Evolution

The New Style

Album Information

A month after the release of my first album, Synchronized, I began experimenting with a new style. The original style showcased in the Synchronized album had the genre of "K-Step," since my name began with a 'K' and I didn't know what other genre it fit into. My new style sounded closer to techno, so I made a side album containing 9 songs with the K-Step Techno genre. I also made "instrumental" versions of these songs, which were the same songs but with less melodies. These were made in case I ever added lyrics to them.
